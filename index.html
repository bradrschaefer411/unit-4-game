Crystal Collector

Here's how you play the game


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Diamond
    • Emerald
    • Ruby
    • Gold
  3. The rules are simple; match the "Number To Get" by clicking away at the gems on screen. If you go over the amount they have, you lose. If you match it, you win!
